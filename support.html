Frequently asked questions
How do I run an SEO audit? ▼
Navigate to the page you want to audit in Chrome, then click the RankSorcery icon in your browser toolbar. Hit the Run Audit button and results will appear within a few seconds.
The extension icon doesn't appear in my toolbar — what do I do? ▼
Click the puzzle piece icon (🧩) in your Chrome toolbar to see all installed extensions. Find RankSorcery and click the pin icon to keep it visible in your toolbar at all times.
Why does the audit say "Unable to analyze this page"? ▼
Some pages restrict content scripts — this includes Chrome internal pages (like chrome://), the Chrome Web Store itself, and some pages that block extensions. Try it on a regular website.
What does the SEO score mean? ▼
The score (0–100) reflects how well the page follows SEO best practices across meta tags, headings, structured data, page speed signals, and more. A score above 80 is strong. Below 50 indicates significant issues worth fixing.
Does RankSorcery send my data anywhere? ▼
No. RankSorcery analyzes pages entirely within your browser. No page data, URLs, or personal information is sent to any external server. Your audits stay private on your device.
Is RankSorcery free? ▼
Yes — RankSorcery is completely free to use. Install it from the Chrome Web Store and run unlimited audits at no cost.
How do I uninstall the extension? ▼
Right-click the RankSorcery icon in your toolbar and select Remove from Chrome. Or go to chrome://extensions, find RankSorcery, and click Remove.
