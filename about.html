Our Mission

Democratizing SEO — Free for Everyone, Forever

Professional SEO tools have always been expensive. Ahrefs starts at $129/month. Semrush at $139/month. Moz at $99/month. For small business owners, indie bloggers, freelancers, and developers in emerging markets, these costs are simply out of reach — even though the SEO knowledge behind them is not.

RankSorcery was built to change that. The belief is simple: the quality of your SEO analysis should not depend on the size of your budget. Every website owner deserves to know if their meta tags are wrong, if their Core Web Vitals are failing, if their schema markup is missing, and exactly how to fix it — for free.

That's why RankSorcery is free forever. No trial periods. No freemium tiers. No paywalled features. Every tool in the RankSorcery toolkit is 100% free, no account required, no credit card, no login. You paste a URL and get a full 80-factor SEO audit in about 30 seconds.

Since launching in 2024, RankSorcery has grown to 25+ free SEO and AI tools, serving tens of thousands of website owners monthly. The platform supports WordPress, Shopify, Wix, Webflow, Squarespace, and 25+ more CMSs, providing platform-specific fix instructions that generic tools simply cannot offer.

Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of optimizing websites so they are cited and surfaced by AI-powered search engines — including Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT Search, Perplexity AI, Claude, Microsoft Copilot, and others. Unlike traditional SEO (which targets blue-link rankings), GEO targets AI-generated answer snippets. Key GEO signals include: structured data (JSON-LD), factual prose, clear entity definitions, E-E-A-T, authoritative third-party mentions, and machine-readable content. RankSorcery's AI Search Visibility Tracker specifically analyzes your GEO readiness and provides recommendations to improve AI citability.
